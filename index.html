The Chef

A Passion for
Exceptional Food

Emily is an accomplished private chef celebrated for her authentic, healthful, and exquisitely crafted fine dining. Drawing upon the rich traditions of British, French, and Mediterranean cuisines, she brings a wealth of expertise and artistry to every plate.

Trained at Eastbourne College of Food and Fashion and holder of an advanced Cordon Bleu qualification, Emily honed her craft as sous chef alongside a Michelin-starred chef at the renowned Chalets de Philippe in Chamonix. Her career has taken her across the globe, cooking for distinguished clients including the President Director of Issey Miyake, the owner of Monsoon International, and members of the Royal Family.

Her most prestigious engagement saw her creating exquisite canapés for one hundred guests at the Issey Miyake new collection launch in London.

Emily's unwavering commitment to organically sourced, locally produced ingredients ensures that every dish is not only remarkably flavoursome but distinctly her own. Every event is transformed into an unforgettable culinary experience, complete with beautifully presented dishes and outstanding service.
